Robotics & Machine Learning Researcher

Welcome to my academic portfolio. I’m Tongshu Wu, a Computer Science Engineering undergraduate at Lehigh University with a minor in Data Science. My academic and research journey is grounded in a deep curiosity about intelligent systems and a commitment to advancing swarm robotics and machine learning for real-world impact.

I currently serve as a Research Assistant at Lehigh's Swarm Lab, where I develop autonomous aerial systems with applications in transportation and collaborative object manipulation. My work spans hardware design, control systems, and algorithm development, and has contributed to a forthcoming IEEE T-RO publication.

In industry, I’ve applied my skills in machine learning and data classification at WAYHO Technology, working with hyperspectral imagery to support law enforcement. These experiences have sharpened my ability to translate research into practical tools — a perspective I carry into all my projects.

This site serves as a gateway to my resume, projects, publications, and ongoing research. I am actively seeking opportunities to contribute to collaborative, innovation-driven research teams at the PhD level.
